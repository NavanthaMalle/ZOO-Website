Events & Activities

The Dolphin Show

Date: Every Weekday

Time: 10:00 AM

Location: The Oceans Gate

Join our knowledgeable zookeepers as they share fascinating facts about dolphins and talents.

Bird Show

Date: Every Weekday

Time: 11:00 AM and 2:00 PM

Location: Bird Park

Witness the beauty and intelligence of various bird species as they showcase their natural behaviors and tricks.

Elephants Drama

Date: Every Tuesday To Friday

Time: 2:30 PM

Location: The Elephant Enclosure

Come see our gentle giants up close and learn about their feeding habits from our expert elephant keepers.

Moon Safari

Date: Every Weekday

Time: 6:00 PM to 10:00 PM

Location: Zawana Grounds

Experience the thrill of a nighttime safari as you explore the zoo after hours and discover the fascinating nocturnal behavior of various animal species.
